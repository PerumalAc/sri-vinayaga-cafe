Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Welcome to Sri Vinayaga South Indian Cafe — where tradition meets taste. For over four decades, we've been serving the finest South Indian cuisine, prepared with hand-ground spices, fresh ingredients, and recipes passed down through generations.

From our signature crispy dosas to aromatic biryanis, every dish is a celebration of flavors that transport you to the heart of South India.
